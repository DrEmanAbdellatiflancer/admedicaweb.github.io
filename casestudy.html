Vocera is a recognized leader in healthcare communication solutions. Their flagship product, the Vocera Smart Badge, is designed to enable seamless, hands-free communication among healthcare teams, improving efficiency and patient care.

The Challenge

Vocera is a recognized leader in healthcare
communication solutions. Their flagship product, the Vocera Smart Badge, is designed to enable seamless, hands-free communication among healthcare teams, improving efficiency and patient care.

Key Challenges

  • English-only marketing collateral was not effectively reaching the Arabic-speaking audience.
  • Materials included brochures, reports, and high-production commercial videos.
  • No original project files were provided—AdMedica had to reconstruct everything from scratch.

Our Approach

Vocera is a recognized leader in healthcare communication solutions. Their flagship product, the Vocera Smart Badge, is designed to enable seamless, hands-free communication among healthcare teams, improving efficiency and patient care.

Process Breakdown

Transcript & Translation

Extracted transcripts and translated content into culturally relevant Arabic.

Voiceover & Dubbing

Recorded professional Arabic voiceovers, maintaining speech cadence and clarity.

Sound Design

Re-engineered audio to align with dubbed content and enhance the narrative.

Visual Re-creation

Redesigned materials in Arabic while preserving layout and branding.

The Solution

AdMedica provided a tailored localization
strategy for Vocera

Brochures & Documents

Translated and redesigned layouts to ensure Arabic versions matched the original’s visual appeal and branding.

Video Content

Adapted videos from infographics to commercials by:

  • Translating on-screen text.
  • Dubbing narration with high-quality voiceovers.
  • Enhancing sound design for authenticity.

Every element was rebuilt to create marketing materials seamlessly
adapted for the Arabic market.

AdMedica brought this story to life, showing how Vocera empowers teams when every heartbeat counts.

Through localization, Vocera’s emergency response message reached audiences with clarity and impact.

From voice to visuals, Vocera’s story was reimagined for the region, making innovation speak Arabic.
